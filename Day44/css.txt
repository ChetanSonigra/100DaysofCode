--> Box Model:

Margin, padding, and border

height: 600px -- pushes elements below it to lower.
width: 600px -- pushes elements on the right to further right.
width: 100%

border: 10px solid black;  - thickness, style(solid,dashed), color
border-top: 0px - to remove top border
border-width: 0px 10px 20px 10px - top,right,bottom,left
border-width: 0px 20px - top,bottom = 0 left,right= 20
border comes outside element

padding: adds space between border and element.
padding: 20px

margin: adds space outside border space
margin: 10px
padding and margin also can take 4/2 values similar to border
